# Assessment Disclaimer — Draft

> **DRAFT — requires review by qualified legal counsel before use.** Not legal advice.
> Shown to students (and visible to parents) at assessment start and on the results screen, and
> **acknowledged** before/after the assessment. Bilingual (ka + en) final required.

## Purpose
Make unambiguous that the platform is a **developmental career-guidance tool**, not a clinical or
diagnostic instrument, and that results are **exploratory**. ⓘ The app's AI/domain layers already
use non-deterministic, "your responses suggest…" wording; this disclaimer makes that a first-class,
recorded acknowledgment.

## Draft text (illustrative — counsel to finalize)

**English**
> This is a **career-guidance tool only**. It is **not** a clinical, psychological, medical, or
> diagnostic assessment, and it does **not** label or diagnose you. Your results are **exploratory**
> — they suggest interests and strengths you might want to explore, not fixed conclusions about who
> you are or what you must become. Please **discuss your results with a counselor, parent/guardian,
> or teacher**.
