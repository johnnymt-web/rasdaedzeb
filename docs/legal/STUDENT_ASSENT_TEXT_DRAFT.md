## 1. Age-appropriate explanation (illustrative)
> "You're about to answer some questions about what you enjoy and what you're good at. There are
> **no right or wrong answers**, and it's **not a test you pass or fail**. We use your answers to
> show you ideas about subjects and careers you might want to **explore**."

## 2. What the platform does
- Suggests directions and strengths to explore.
- Helps you and your counselor/parent talk about your future.
- Keeps your answers private and only shared with the people allowed to help you (your counselor,
  your parent, your school).

## 3. What the platform does NOT do
- It is **not** a medical, psychological, or clinical test.
- It does **not** diagnose you or **decide** your future.
- It does **not** tell you "you must become X."

## 4. Student rights & support
- You can **ask questions** at any time.
- You can talk to a **trusted adult, your counselor, or your parent** about your results.
